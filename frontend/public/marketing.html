Frequently Asked Questions

Everything contractors ask us about AI receptionists

Will customers know it's AI?
+
Nope. Our AI sounds exactly like a professional receptionist thanks to ElevenLabs voice technology. Customers can't tell the difference. It handles conversations naturally, asks follow-up questions, and even picks up on emotional cues.
What if I get an emergency call?
+
You can set keywords like "emergency," "leak," "broken pipe," or "no heat" to forward directly to your cell phone. Your AI knows when to escalate urgent situations and when to handle routine inquiries on its own.
Can it give estimates over the phone?
+
It can provide ballpark price ranges that you configure (e.g., "Kitchen remodels typically range from $15K-$50K depending on scope"). For detailed estimates, it books an on-site consultation and collects all the details you need before you arrive.
Does it speak Spanish (or other languages)?
+
Yes! We support 25+ languages including Spanish, which is perfect for areas with diverse customer bases. Your AI can switch languages mid-conversation and speak fluently with any caller.
How long does setup take?
+
Most contractors are live in under 5 minutes. You either get a new phone number or forward your existing line, tell the AI about your services and pricing in a simple conversation, and you're done. No technical skills required.
What if a customer asks something my AI doesn't know?
+
Your AI will gracefully say "That's a great question - let me have [your name] call you back within an hour to discuss that in detail." It then sends you a text with the caller's info and their question so you can follow up.
