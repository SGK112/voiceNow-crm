## ðŸŽ¯ Customer Segmentation Analysis

### **Segment 1: Enterprise/Agency (20% of customers, 60% of revenue)**
**Profile:**
- High volume (1,000+ calls/month)
- Need workflows, multiple agents, CRM
- Predictable usage
- Want dedicated support

**Best Pricing:** Enterprise Subscription ($999/mo)
- 5,000 minutes included
- Unlimited agents
- All features
- **Break-even:** If they use >5,000 min/mo, subscriptions save money vs credits

---

### **Segment 2: Growing Business (50% of customers, 30% of revenue)**
**Profile:**
- 200-1,000 calls/month
- Need CRM + automation
- Growing but budget-conscious
- Seasonal fluctuations

**Best Pricing:** Professional Subscription ($299/mo) + Credit Top-ups
- Use subscription for base usage
- Buy credits for overflow/busy seasons
- **Hybrid approach saves money**

---

### **Segment 3: Small Business/Freelancer (30% of customers, 10% of revenue)**
**Profile:**
- <200 calls/month
- Sporadic usage
- Price-sensitive
- DIY setup

**Best Pricing:** Credit Packages (Professional Pack $149)
- 2,000 minutes = 13 months at 150 min/mo
- No recurring commitment
- **Much cheaper** than any subscription
